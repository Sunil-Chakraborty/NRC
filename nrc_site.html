Over 50 Years of Engineering Precision in Bulk Material Handling

Founded in 1973, NRC Industries Limited has grown into one of India's foremost manufacturers of conveyor belts and advanced bulk material handling products, serving key sectors such as mining, power, ports, steel, fertilizers, chemicals, and cement. With captive fabric weaving and dipping facilities, NRC operates a vertically integrated manufacturing ecosystem that ensures consistency, customization, and quality at scale.

With in-house R&D and rigorous quality control, we manufacture belts and accessories across a wide range of tensile strengths, cover compounds, and constructions, built to perform in the most demanding environments across India and globally. All solutions are engineered and tested to meet or exceed international standards including DIN, ISO, BIS, MSHA, CAN/CSA, and REACH
